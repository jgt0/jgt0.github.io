Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, 222 Coffee House is more than just a café—it’s a welcoming escape where the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets the lively buzz of community connections. Our name reflects not only our address but also our philosophy: to be your second home and second wind, where every visit feels like a revitalizing pause in your day. From the moment you step inside, our cozy, thoughtfully designed space invites you to slow down, savor, and stay a while.

At 222 Coffee House, we take coffee seriously because we know you do too. Our beans are handpicked from sustainable growers around the world, then roasted locally to highlight their distinct flavors and profiles. Every cup is crafted with care by our skilled baristas, who are passionate about making your go-to drink perfect every time. Whether you love a velvety latte, a bold espresso, or a refreshing cold brew, we have something to awaken your senses. To complement your coffee, we offer a curated selection of locally sourced pastries, artisanal snacks, and seasonal treats that are as delightful as the beverages they accompany.

But we’re more than just what’s on the menu—we’re a place to gather, dream, and recharge. Our café is designed for everyone, from students seeking a quiet study spot with reliable Wi-Fi to professionals looking for a creative workspace or friends catching up over cappuccinos. We also host events that bring our vibrant community together, from open mic nights to coffee-tasting workshops. At 222 Coffee House, we’re dedicated to serving more than just coffee; we’re here to brew moments that matter and foster connections that last. Come join us and discover why we’re San Luis Obispo’s favorite place to sip, savor, and stay.
